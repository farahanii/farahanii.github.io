What is exactly a "Family" to you?

"Family" isn't defined only by last name or by blood, it is defined by commitment and by love. It means showing up when they need it the most. It means having each other's backs. It means choosing to love each other eventhough on those days when you struggle to like each other. It mean never giving up with one and other.
